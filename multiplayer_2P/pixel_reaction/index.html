PIXEL REACTION

HOW TO PLAY

- 1v1 turn-based strategy on a 6x6 grid.

- Click an empty cell or your own cell to add a pixel.

- When a cell gets 4 pixels, it POPS!

- Pops spread pixels up, down, left, right, claiming enemy cells.

- Chain reactions are key. Wipe out the enemy color to win!
